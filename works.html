Works

 


The Obex EP

Intended to bring you deep into the mind of 'Obex'—a vile
creature sprouted from the void—whose sole purpose
is to deceive it's visitors in the most malicious of ways.
Journey through in this electrifying 10 minute mini-EP.

("The Obex" is an EP release from February of 2016.)

 



Crooked Delight LP

In this story, you shall venture through a
futuristic, "lo-fidelity" 6th century England.
It's intended to get more and more intense as you go
further and further in—it's quite bizarre, really!
Definitely worth a listen if you are interested
in the abstract art world.

("Crooked Delight" is an LP release from August of 2015.)

 



Stygian EP

Feel the wrath of Hades & his
screech owl known as 'Ascalaphus'.

("Stygian" is an EP release from December of 2015.)
